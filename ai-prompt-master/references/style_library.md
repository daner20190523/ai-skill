# AI 绘图风格库

## 艺术风格（Art Styles）

### 传统绘画
- **油画** oil painting, impasto texture, rich brushstrokes
- **水彩** watercolor, soft washes, bleeding edges, wet-on-wet
- **水墨画** ink wash painting, sumi-e, black ink on rice paper, minimalist brushwork
- **工笔画** Chinese meticulous painting, fine linework, layered colors, silk texture
- **浮世绘** ukiyo-e, Japanese woodblock print, flat colors, bold outlines
- **素描** pencil sketch, charcoal drawing, cross-hatching, grayscale
- **版画** woodcut print, linocut, engraving, high contrast
- **壁画** fresco, mural style, matte texture, aged patina
- **敦煌壁画** Dunhuang mural, weathered mineral pigments, Buddhist motifs, oxidized surfaces
- **青绿山水** blue-green landscape, mineral pigments, Tang dynasty style

### 现代/数字
- **赛博朋克** cyberpunk, neon lights, rain-slicked streets, holographic displays
- **蒸汽朋克** steampunk, brass gears, Victorian aesthetics, steam-powered machinery
- **极简主义** minimalist, clean lines, negative space, geometric forms
- **波普艺术** pop art, Ben-Day dots, bold colors, comic book style
- **超现实主义** surrealism, dreamlike, melting objects, impossible geometry
- **低多边形** low poly, faceted surfaces, geometric simplicity, game art
- **像素艺术** pixel art, 8-bit, 16-bit, retro gaming aesthetic
- **故障艺术** glitch art, digital corruption, RGB shift, data moshing
- **蒸汽波** vaporwave, retro-futuristic, pastel neon, marble statues, 80s-90s nostalgia
- **3D渲染** 3D render, octane render, C4D, raytracing, photorealistic

### 摄影/电影
- **胶片摄影** analog film, 35mm, grain, light leak, Kodak Portra
- **黑白摄影** black and white photography, Ansel Adams zone system, silver gelatin
- **电影质感** cinematic, anamorphic lens, shallow depth of field, film grain
- **纪实摄影** documentary photography, photojournalism, candid, natural light
- **宝丽来** Polaroid, instant film, vintage border, soft focus
- **微距摄影** macro photography, extreme close-up, shallow DOF, bokeh
- **航拍** aerial photography, drone shot, bird's eye view, top-down

### 插画/设计
- **扁平设计** flat design, vector art, bold colors, clean shapes
- **线稿** line art, black and white, clean outlines, no shading
- **剪纸风格** paper cut art, layered paper, shadow depth, craft aesthetic
- **涂鸦** graffiti, street art, spray paint, urban wall, bold tags
- **绘本风格** children's book illustration, storybook, whimsical, warm tones
- **漫画** manga, anime style, cel shading, dynamic composition, screen tone
- **厚涂** thick paint, impasto, heavy texture, palette knife strokes
- **概念艺术** concept art, keyframe, moody lighting, cinematic composition

### 东方美学
- **水墨丹青** Chinese ink and color, xieyi style, poetic atmosphere
- **和风** Japanese traditional, wabi-sabi, zen garden, minimal elegance
- **敦煌风** Dunhuang style, flying apsaras, bodhisattva, mural texture
- **青花瓷** blue and white porcelain, cobalt blue, ceramic glaze, delicate patterns
- **皮影戏** shadow puppet, translucent leather, silhouette, warm backlight

## 光影关键词库

### 自然光
- **golden hour** - warm golden light, long shadows, magical atmosphere
- **blue hour** - twilight blue, serene, before dawn/after sunset
- **soft diffused light** - overcast, even lighting, no harsh shadows
- **god rays / crepuscular rays** - sunbeams through clouds or trees
- **backlight / rim light** - subject outlined by light, halo effect
- **dappled light** - light filtering through leaves,斑点光影
- **harsh midday sun** - strong contrast, deep shadows, bright highlights
- **moonlight** - cool blue, silver tones, night atmosphere

### 人工光
- **neon lighting** - vibrant artificial colors, cyberpunk, night scenes
- **cinematic lighting** - three-point lighting, dramatic shadows, film noir
- **volumetric lighting** - visible light beams, atmospheric haze, fog light
- **candlelight** - warm flickering, intimate, golden orange glow
- **studio lighting** - controlled, professional, product photography
- **bioluminescence** - glowing organisms, magical, underwater, fantasy
- **firelight** - warm orange, dancing shadows, campfire atmosphere
- **holographic light** - iridescent, rainbow refractions, futuristic

### 特殊光效
- **lens flare** - optical artifact, cinematic, JJ Abrams style
- **bokeh** - out-of-focus light circles, dreamy background
- **light leak** - vintage film effect, warm color bleed
- **chiaroscuro** - strong light-dark contrast, Rembrandt style
- **ethereal glow** - soft magical radiance, fantasy atmosphere
- **sparkle / glitter** - point reflections, magical particles
- **silhouette** - subject in shadow against bright background
- **double exposure light** - layered light effects, dreamy overlap

## 色彩关键词库

### 色调方案
- **monochromatic** - single color family, elegant unity
- **complementary** - opposite colors, vibrant contrast (blue-orange, purple-yellow)
- **analogous** - adjacent colors, harmonious flow
- **triadic** - three evenly spaced colors, balanced vibrancy
- **split-complementary** - one base + two adjacent to complement
- **pastel** - soft, muted, dreamy, low saturation
- **jewel tones** - deep rich colors, emerald, sapphire, ruby
- **earth tones** - warm browns, ochre, terracotta, olive
- **neon palette** - electric, vibrant, cyberpunk, synthwave
- **muted / desaturated** - subdued, moody, atmospheric
- **high contrast** - bold black and white with selective color
- **sepia** - vintage brown, nostalgic, old photograph
- **iridescent** - rainbow sheen, holographic, pearlescent
- **noir** - high contrast black and white, shadows dominant

### 具体色彩组合
- **warm palette**: golden, amber, crimson, burnt orange
- **cool palette**: teal, navy, ice blue, silver
- **sunset**: coral, lavender, tangerine, rose gold
- **forest**: emerald, moss green, bark brown, dappled gold
- **ocean**: deep blue, turquoise, seafoam, aquamarine
- **autumn**: rust, mustard, burgundy, pumpkin
- **winter**: ice white, pale blue, silver gray, frost
- **spring**: cherry blossom pink, fresh green, sky blue
- **cyberpunk**: magenta, cyan, electric purple, acid green
- **vintage film**: warm amber, faded teal, cream

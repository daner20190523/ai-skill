# 签名风格库

## 个性签名（Signature Tags）

个性签名是提示词末尾的"调味料"，赋予画面独特的审美倾向和平台辨识度。

### Midjourney 系
- `--ar 16:9 --style raw --s 750`
- `--ar 3:4 --style expressive --s 250`
- `--ar 1:1 --style cute --s 500`
- `--ar 2:3 --style scenic --s 400`
- `--ar 9:16 --chaos 20 --weird 50`
- `--ar 16:9 --niji 6`
- `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1`

### Stable Diffusion 系
- `(masterpiece, best quality:1.2), (highly detailed:1.1)`
- `trending on ArtStation, deviantart`
- `trending on Civitai, (award-winning:1.1)`
- `8k wallpaper, ultra detailed`
- `(photorealistic:1.3), RAW photo, 85mm lens`
- `concept art, digital painting, (intricate details:1.2)`

### DALL·E 系
- `digital art, trending on artstation`
- `4K, high quality, detailed`
- `cinematic lighting, professional`

### 通用增强标签
- `masterpiece, best quality, award-winning`
- `ultra detailed, 8K, high resolution`
- `trending on ArtStation, CGSociety`
- `professional, commercial photography`
- `studio lighting, sharp focus`

### 氛围签名
- `ethereal atmosphere, dreamlike quality`
- `haunting beauty, melancholic mood`
- `epic cinematic, dramatic atmosphere`
- `serene and peaceful, zen aesthetic`
- `dark and moody, film noir`
- `whimsical and magical, fairy tale`

### 负面提示词（Negative Prompt 参考）
- `blurry, low quality, distorted, deformed, ugly, bad anatomy`
- `extra limbs, missing fingers, fused fingers, too many fingers`
- `watermark, text, signature, logo, frame`
- `lowres, jpeg artifacts, grain, noise`
- `crooked, asymmetric, disfigured, mutation`
